Fabric Care Guide

A quick reference for washing, drying, ironing, and storing premium mid-weight to heavy-weight fabrics and insulated garments — helping your end consumers maintain quality and longevity.

Fabric / Fill Type Washing Drying Ironing Special Notes
Wool / Cashmere / Alpaca / Mohair Dry clean preferred / Cold hand wash Lay flat to dry Low heat, use pressing cloth Avoid agitation; store folded
Double-Faced Wool Dry clean only Lay flat Low heat, press on wrong side Professional care recommended
Down / Feather Fill Machine wash gentle / Cold Tumble dry low with tennis balls Do not iron Ensure fully dry to prevent clumping
Synthetic Down (Primaloft / Thinsulate) Machine wash gentle / Warm Tumble dry low Do not iron Do not use fabric softener
Down-Proof / Puffer Shell Machine wash gentle / Cold Tumble dry low Do not iron Zip all closures before washing
Performance / Membrane (Softshell / Hardshell) Machine wash gentle / No fabric softener Air dry or tumble low Do not iron Reapply DWR coating as needed
Fleece (Micro / Polar / Sherpa) Machine wash / Cold Tumble dry low Do not iron Wash inside out to reduce pilling
Denim Machine wash inside out / Cold Air dry Warm iron Wash sparingly to preserve color
Heavy Cotton / Ripstop Machine wash / Warm Tumble dry medium High heat if needed Expect slight shrinkage on first wash
Wool Batting / Cotton Batting Dry clean recommended Lay flat Low heat Avoid machine washing
Kapok / Hemp Fill Dry clean only — — Natural fibers; professional care only
Sorona / Bio-based Fill Machine wash gentle / Cold Tumble dry low Do not iron Follow synthetic down guidelines
