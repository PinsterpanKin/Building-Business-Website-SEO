Craft & Quality Control

Precision in every stitch. Control at every stage. From pattern making to final inspection, every garment we produce is built on decades of hands-on experience and a system designed for consistency.

1. Specialized Craftsmanship

Outerwear & Wool Coats Double-Faced Wool Joinery: Hand-stitched seams that open flat — no lining needed.
Collar & Lapel Shaping: Precision fusing and pressing for a crisp roll.
Bound Buttonholes: Hand-finished for a premium look.
Down Jackets & Puffers Baffle Box Construction: Inner fabric walls keep down evenly distributed.
Down-Proof Stitching: Optimized needle size/density to prevent leakage.
Fill Power Control: Precision filling by weight per chamber.
Pants & Trousers Reinforced Crotch Seam: Double-stitched/taped for durability on heavy fabrics.
Waistband Construction: Interlining for shape retention, clean edges.
Pleat & Crease Setting: Permanent crease setting for trousers.
Technical Outerwear Taped Seams & Laser Cutting: Fully sealed for waterproof construction.
Vent & Pocket Engineering: Functional details positioned for ergonomics.

2. Our 4-Stage Quality Control System (AQL 2.5 / 4.0)

1
Incoming QC
Fabric 4-point check, down fill power testing, trims verification.
2
In-Process QC
First article approval & inline checks every 2 hours.
3
Final QC
100% visual inspection, measurement audit, needle detection.
4
Pre-Shipment QC
AQL random sampling, carton weight, third-party verification.

3. Material & Garment Testing Standards

Category Testing Metrics & Standards
Down & Fill Testing Fill Power: IDFB / EN 12130 (500–900+ FP)
Cleanliness: Oxygen Number ≤ 10, Turbidity ≥ 500 mm
Certification: RDS (Responsible Down Standard) available.
Fabric Testing Shrinkage: AATCC 135 / ISO 6330
Seam Slippage: ASTM D434 / ISO 13936
Performance: Hydrostatic head, MVTR for waterproof/breathable.
Third-Party & Audits We welcome SGS, Intertek, Bureau Veritas for on-site production or pre-shipment inspections. Live video and remote inspection support available.
