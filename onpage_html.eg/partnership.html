Our OEM Process & Business Guidelines

We build long-term relationships based on transparency, quality, and mutual growth. Understand our 8-step OEM workflow and manufacturing guidelines to start your premium outerwear production.

01
Inquiry & Consultation
  • Review design direction, market, and target cost.
  • Confirm preferred fabric options.
  • Assess sample reference and production difficulty.
02
Quotation & Sampling
  • Sample development fees may be credited against bulk orders.
  • First sample checks fit and proportion.
  • Revisions lock size, fabric, and trims.
03
Order & Contract
  • Confirm specs, quantities, and pricing.
  • Lock payment terms and delivery schedule.
  • Set inspection standards before production.
04
Material Sourcing & Pre-Production
  • Inspect incoming fabrics for hidden defects.
  • Complete pre-shrinking or stability treatment.
  • Prepare compatible trims and supporting materials.
05
Bulk Production
  • Control fabric tension during cutting.
  • Calibrate machines for fine fabrics.
  • Check key stages during production.
06
Quality Control
  • Inspect appearance and workmanship.
  • Check functional details and finishing.
  • Confirm packing quality before shipment.
07
Packing & Shipment
  • Prepare hanging or flat-pack options.
  • Add labels and presentation details.
  • Coordinate shipping documents and transport.
08
After-Sales Service & Ongoing Partnership
  • Technical files retained for repeat orders.
  • Questions answered within 24 hours.
  • Objective is long-term coordination, not one-time delivery.

Business Guidelines

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) 300 pcs per style per color. We are flexible with smaller test runs for new clients, though a sampling surcharge may apply.
Sample Lead Time 7–14 days. Managed directly by our Guangzhou technical team to ensure rapid iteration and perfect pattern making.
Bulk Production Lead Time 45–60 days (depending on fabric availability and complexity). Down and double-faced wool garments require slower, precision-focused production.
Quality Standard AQL 2.5 / 4.0. We enforce a strict 4-stage inspection process (Incoming, In-process, Final, Pre-shipment). We welcome third-party inspections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you source specific down insulation and premium wool for our brand?
Yes. Our sourcing team has deep relationships with top-tier suppliers across Inner Mongolia, Shandong, and Jiangsu. We can procure RDS-certified down (up to 900+ Fill Power), cashmere, and technical performance fabrics.
Do you sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)?
Absolutely. We strictly protect our clients' intellectual property. Your designs, tech packs, and patterns will remain completely confidential and will not be shared or reproduced for other parties.
Where will my garments be produced?
Sampling, cutting, finishing, and QA are strictly handled at our Guangzhou HQ. Bulk sewing is allocated between our Guangzhou factory and our long-term partner facilities in Jiangxi and Sichuan, ensuring competitive pricing without compromising quality.
